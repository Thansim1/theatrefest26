Crescent Team Dramatics

1984 Founded as Crescent Drama Team, marking the beginning of a rich theatrical journey. 2006 Evolved into the official Crescent Variety Team, expanding creative boundaries. 2009 Renamed as Crescent Team Dramatix (CTD), establishing a strong and unified identity. 2010 Introduced Technical Performances — UV, Shadow, LED acts, and Live Band shows — redefining stage innovation. 2010 - 2024 Consistently emerged as winners in Inter-Collegiate and Intra-Collegiate competitions, showcasing excellence year after year. Today CTD Stands as one of South india's most consistentand evolving dramatics teams,carrying a powerful legacy of performance,innovation,and impact.
